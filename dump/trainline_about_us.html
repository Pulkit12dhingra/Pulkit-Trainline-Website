Who we are

We are Europes leading train and coach app. To put it simply, we are a one-stop-shop for train and coach travel. Every day, we gather routes, prices, and travel times from over 270 rail and coach operators in 40 countries, so that everyone can buy tickets quickly and save time, effort, and money.

We stay one stepahead,soall of our customers dont have to,we are making it easier for them to buy the best ticket for their journeywith giving them live, personalised travel information on the go via ourawesome app. We offer a comprehensive collection of travel options to our customers, as well as unique, AI-driven information tohelp them make the best decisions.

Our mission

We are Europes leading train and coach app. Our aim is to bring together all rail, coach and other travel services into one simple experience so people can get the best prices and smart, real-time travel info on the go.

What we do

Trainline currently operates through the following three segments: 

UK Consumer

We sell rail tickets on behalf of all UK rail companies and coach tickets on behalf of National Express, to customers worldwide.

Through our highly rated mobile app, customers benefit from having all carriers, fares and their digital railcard in one place, real time travel information on-the-go, and self-serve after sales functionality.

We provide high-quality, relevant recommendations for ancillary services like trip insurance and partner with carefully selected businesses and brands wishing to advertise on our mobile app and website.

International Consumer

Our International Consumer business sells rail tickets to people all over the world on behalf of all the major European rail companies. Our International Consumer business also sells coach tickets on behalf of all the major European coach companies.

European rail markets are becoming more fragmented, following the EUs Fourth Railway Package, which from December 2020 opened domestic rail passenger services to competition. As new entrant rail carriers launch services on newly liberalised routes, we give customers an easy way to compare all carriers, fares and journey options in our highly rated app.

Trainline Solutions

Trainline Solutions combines our Platform One technology with our Trainline Partner Solutions business.

Trainline Partner Solutions provides retailing capabilities for carriers, businesses and travel sellers.

Trainline Partner Solutions offers travel management companies and other travel platforms access to all rail carriers in one place, through one simple connection, as well as rail booking solutions directly to thousands of small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs), large corporate entities and public sector partners.

Trainline Partner Solutions also builds, supports and manages white label online and mobile sales platforms for many of the UKs biggest rail companies. It leverages Trainline's proprietary technology to provide rail companies with a best-in-class product and a lower cost to serve compared to developing their own ticket sales solutions.

Our history

1997

 

Trainline is set upby the Virgin Groupwith the mission of helping customers save money on train tickets. At first, wedidnt sell tickets on theweb;only overa telephone line. The train line.

2008 

We enabled customers to print their train tickets at home for the first time.It was ground-breaking then, butan industry standardnow. 

2009

We launched our first iPhone app, beginning a new phase ofdevelopment and mobile-friendly focused product features includingeticketsand live Journey Planner.(Were not bragging, but wereckonour apps are stillamongstthe very best!) 

2010

We were awarded an international licence by the UKs Association of TrainOperating Carriers(ATOC)to retail UK ticketsto internationalcustomers. Thats meant thatpeople all over the world are now able to travelby train in the UKeven more easily than before. 

2016

We acquiredthe Paris-basedCaptain Train,combining two of Europes leading digital rail platforms to create the new leader in the European market.We also expanded into the coach market, forming supply partnerships with IC Bus, ALSA and National Express meaning customers have travel options to suit every need. As ever we wereinnovating on the tech front too with the launch ofPriceBot. Customers can use this nifty app feature to see the cheapest ticket available on their route for that day. 

2017

By 2017,its fair to say that our global journeyhad really begun. We had localised apps in the UK, France, Germany, Italy and Spain anddeepened our Central Eastern rail network with aLeoExpresspartnership.We then launched 11 new websites, nineadditional languages (14 total) and pricesweredisplayed in 15 new currencies. 

More innovations were launched, like Price Prediction indicating when a ticket will bethe best value, helping customers identify the best time to book. 

2018

More than 50% ofourticket salesin the UK for the first time were mobile-based tickets, showing just how popular theyve become.

We introduced theUKs mostadvanced rail voice AI:Trainlines Voice App withGoogle Assistant. We also made our first steps into Asia withaJTB partnership.

2019

Trainline was floated on the London Stock Exchange at 8 am on 21st June 2019 under the Ticker symbol TRN. The business soon joining the FTSE 250 index of mid-cap firms.
